How to Play Doctor Puzzle:

  • Invite your friends to complete the team of doctors to save lives
  • To score, match at least 3 pieces vertically or horizontally of the item needed for the cure
  • You have 1 minute to score enough points of the right items to save the patient
  • At a given time, only one item combination will give you points. This item will be shown to only one player, so communicate doctors!
  • Pay attention to the patient's heart beats. Only your skills combined can save his life!
